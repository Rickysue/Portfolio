Personal Project

Online-Coursera, 2020.06-2020.07

Business Problem:
Where is the best place to open a coffee shop business in around Universities in Beijing?

Data

- List of 83 universities in Beijing
- Latitude and Longtitude of these universities
- Venue data of coffee shops around universities

Methodology

- Gaode Map API to get the geographical coordinates
- Foursquare API to get the top 100 venues within a radisu of 2km.
- Filter the "Coffee Shop" and used K-menas to indentify the concentration density of Coffee shops around the universities.
- Five clusters: no exitence to low number,moderate number, moderate concentration, relatively high concentration, high concentration.

Results and Discussion:

- Coffee shops in Haidian are concentrated in office builidings and shopping malls since it is a college-city distrcit with developed business enviornment that is not far from universities. So we can start the business inside the university or near the university entrance

- Brands like Starbucks, Pacific, Costa has carved up the market for the over 30-yuan a cup of coffee. However, the cheap coffee market for students still has the huge opportunity.

- Outside Haidian District is the less developed or developing university-city ares. Coffee business should focus on no only the white-collars but also the students.
